Apart Hotel Alex


When visiting Himare, Apart Hotel Alex is a great choice to consider.
After splashing around at the seasonal outdoor pool, you can unwind with a drink at the pool bar.

Apartments feature kitchenettes, Smart TVs and rainfall showerheads...


Stay once, carry memories forever.





Apart Hotel Alex features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, garden, a terrace and bar in Himare. Located around a 17-minute walk from Spille Beach, the hotel with free WiFi is also one mile away from Maracit Beach. The hotel has family rooms.
Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hairdryer, guest rooms at the hotel have a TV and air conditioning, and selected rooms come with a seating area. At Apart Hotel Alex each room has bed linen and towels.
Speaking Greek, English and Albanian at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.
Prinos Beach is 1.1 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 89 miles from Apart Hotel Alex.
